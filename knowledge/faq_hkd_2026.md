# FAQ_HKD_2026_DETAILED
- **Source:** Google Doc "41 câu hỏi về HKD" (updated 2026-06-12)
- **Content:** Detailed Q&A from Tax Department regarding HKD 2026.

## Key Categories & Insights:

### 1. Accounting & Books (Sổ sách)
- **Revenue $\le$ 500M:** Only 01 book: "Sổ doanh thu bán hàng hóa, dịch vụ" (Mẫu S1a-HKD).
- **500M $<$ Revenue $\le$ 3B (Rate-based tax):** Use "Sổ doanh thu bán hàng hóa, dịch vụ" (Mainly recording revenue).
- **500M $<$ Revenue $\le$ 3B (or $>$ 3B) (Income-based tax):** Use set of 4 books:
    - Sổ doanh thu bán hàng hóa, dịch vụ.
    - Sổ chi tiết doanh thu, chi phí.
    - Sổ chi tiết vật liệu, dụng cụ, sản phẩm, hàng hóa.
    - Sổ chi tiết tiền.
- **Flexibility:** Recording can be per transaction or periodic. Not mandatory to list every item detailed, but recommended.

### 2. Tax Calculation for High Revenue ($>$ 3B)
- **Range 3B - 50B:** Tax on taxable income (Revenue - Expenses) $\times$ 17%.
- **No Invoices for Expenses:** Tax office provides guidelines on creating expense lists (bảng kê chi phí).
- **VAT:** HKD using direct method (revenue $\times$ %) **cannot** deduct input VAT. To deduct, must convert to a Corporate Enterprise.

### 3. E-commerce (TMĐT)
- **Revenue Calculation:** For lottery/Vietlott, revenue = commission only, not total ticket value.
- **Invoicing on Platforms:** Can authorize the platform to issue invoices or issue them manually (per Decree 70/2025).
- **Timing:** Invoice issued at the time of ownership transfer, regardless of payment timing.
- **Platform Fees:** Fees/discounts are part of revenue (if received) or deductible expenses (if paying and having proper invoices).
- **Low Value Orders:** Tax office is researching better solutions for small-value orders (vài nghìn đồng) to reduce costs.

### 4. Real Estate Rental (Cho thuê nhà/sạp)
- **Registration:** Required via one-stop-shop (linked with business reg).
- **Revenue $\le$ 500M:** Not subject to VAT/PIT. Must still notify actual revenue twice a year (by July 31 and Jan 31).
- **Revenue $>$ 500M:** 
    - PIT = (Revenue - 500M) $\times$ 5%.
    - VAT = Revenue $\times$ 5%.
- **Authorized Payment:** Use the information of the contract signer (the owner) for tax filings.

### 5. General HKD Rules
- **Change from Lump-sum (Khoán) to Declaration (Kê khai):** Automatic transition from 01/01/2026. No procedure needed.
- **Business Locations:** Multiple locations use one MST (Personal ID). Must separate revenue for each.
- **Payment Proof:** Bank transfers are payment proof, NOT accounting documents. Invoices/lists are required to determine revenue/expenses.
- **Low Revenue ($<$ 500M) Invoices:** Not mandatory to use e-invoices, but can contact Tax office for guidance if a customer requests one.
- **VAT/PIT Threshold:** 500M/year (below this is not subject to tax, but must report revenue).
